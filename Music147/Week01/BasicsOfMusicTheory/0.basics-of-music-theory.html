Basics of Music Theory

Summary

The "Basics of Music Theory" module consists of five items. The first three are optional, since they will be mostly review for students who have previously taken music classes or private lessons. The last two items are mandatory, as they explain how to represent basic music theoretical concepts within the computer.

Items

  1. Review: The Notes on a Keyboard
  2. Review: Intervals and Scales
  3. Review: Fundamentals of Rhythm
  4. Representing Pitch in the Computer
  5. Representing Rhythm in the Computer
