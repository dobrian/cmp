Musical Timing

Summary

In this topic you will learn about different ways to express and manage the passage of time for musical purposes. You will undertand the difference between Clock Time and Musical Time, review and learn interesting ways to deal with beat and rhythm, and become familiar with the Temporal Grid. After that you will explore ways to put all this knowledge in practice.
