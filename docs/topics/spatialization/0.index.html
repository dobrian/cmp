Sound Spatialization

Prerequisites

Please make sure you are familiar with Physics of Sound and Sampling Theory before starting this topic.

Summary

This topic will try to explain how sound behaves in space and how it is perceived and localized by the listenner. Some techniques to manipulate sound spatialization in digital audio are also approached.
