Self-Study

Let's get back to our Overly Simple MIDI Sythn patch and try to improve it.

  • Try combining some of the things you learned so far — limiting incoming MIDI note data with poly to play only a single note at a time, generate a tone with one of the oscillator objects, generate an amplitude envelope to shape the sound, and consider adding some vibrato to the sound with LFO frequency modulation.
  • How would you add MIDI pitchbend control to vary the pitch of the sound? (Hint: You might want to research in the Max Cookbook and the MSP Tutorials.)
