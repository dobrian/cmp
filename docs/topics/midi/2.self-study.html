Self-Study

  • Write a program in Max that responds to incoming MIDI note-on messages by playing a sound with an appropriate pitch and loudness, based on the key number and velocity information.

Self-Quiz

  1. When was MIDI invented?

    2. In the early 1980s

  2. At what rate is MIDI data transmitted?

    3. 31,250 bits per second

  3. What are the two main categories of MIDI messages?

    4. "system" messages and "channel" messages

  4. Which of those two types is used for most performance information?

    5. Channel messages

  5. What is the term for the first byte of every MIDI message?

    6. The "status" byte, indicating the type of message

  6. What is the numerical range of MIDI status bytes?

    7. 128 to 255 (the first bit is always 1)

  7. In a MIDI note-on message, after the status byte, what follows?

    8. The key number and the velocity

  8. What's one important note attribute that's not included in a MIDI note-on message.

    9. Duration

  9. What does a .mid file contain?

    10. Time-tagged MIDI messages

  10. Is MIDI good for transmitting audio?

    11. No! It is used to transmit performance information.
